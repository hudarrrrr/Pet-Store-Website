About Us

Founded in 2001, Animal Planet, Inc. are the largest stores of services and solutions for the lifetime needs of pets. At Animal Planet, we love pets, and we believe pets make us better people. Every day with every connection, Animal Planet’s passionate associates help bring pet parents closer to their pets so they can live more fulfilled lives. This vision impacts everything we do for our customers the way we support our associates, and how we give back to our communities.

We operate approximately 40 pet stores in Egypt , the United States , france , Saudi Arabia , UAE , Tunisia and Morocco as well as more than 200 in-store Animal Planet PetsHotel dog and cat boarding facilities. Animal Planet provides a broad range of competitively priced products and offers unique pet services including training, pet grooming, boarding, Animal Planet Doggie Day Camp and in-store pet adoptions.

We Animal Planet provide health care to all pets and we take care of them as fully as possible so as to guarantee them a happy and quiet living, and we cooperate with more than 50 thousand organizations around the world to provide all resources for our pets such as food and accessories for them.

Animal Planet partners, with the non-profit Animal Planet Foundation, Animal Planet, collaborate with nearly 2,000 animal welfare organizations to bring adoptable pets into stores so they have the best possible opportunity to find a home forever. Through this in-store adoption program and other signature activities, Animal Planet has facilitated more than 5 million adoptions - more than any other brick and mortar organization.
